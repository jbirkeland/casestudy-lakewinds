November Community Giving

All Square is a professional development institute educating and serving formerly incarcerated individuals through their companion grilled cheese restaurant in Minneapolis. All Square believes that those who have paid their debts to society are ‘all square’ and free to move forward with a clean state. Using their three-phase program, students learn the skills needed to get back into the work force while still being compensated with a living wage. Students also receive mental health and wellness support, and are provided sufficient means for housing and support outside of the All Square program.
