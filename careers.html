Careers

Lakewinds is an independent, locally owned food co-op specializing in local and organic food since 1975. We provide a fun, supportive workplace guided by the seven cooperative principles where everyone can bring their passion into the workplace and make a difference. We’re always looking for people who are passionate about great food, about the communities they live in and about how we treat our planet and our fellow humans. With three stores and an administrative office, we know we have a job that’s just right for you!

Benefits

Lakewinds has a competitive benefits package. Depending on the amount an employee works, benefits include:

  • 15% Discount on purchases
  • Paid time off for full-time employees
  • Paid holidays
  • 401(k) plan with company match
  • Employer-sponsored health and dental insurance
  • Flexible spending plans (medical and dependent care)
  • Gain sharing

We also provide training in natural foods, cooperatives, customer service, workplace safety and more.
